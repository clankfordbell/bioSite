Kalvin Lankford grew up in Seward, Nebraska and now reside in Lincoln, Nebraska. Kalvin is a new father with the birth of his son, Kolton, in September. Kalvin works as a Customer Service Representative at Signature Performance. Kalvin spent time in high school and college competing in pole vault competitions. During Kalvin’s senior year of high school, he placed 2nd in pole vault event in Nebraska State Activities Association. Kalvin continues to be an avid sports fan. When Kalvin has free time, he likes to spend time with his son, produce music, and play golf. Kalvin loves to cheer on his favorite sports team, The Nebraska Cornhuskers. Kalvin has always enjoyed music. He often would wonder why he enjoys this music or dislikes this music. Kalvin started learning music theory and this led to him producing his own music soon after. Kalvin started writing and producing his own music in 2022. Kalvin started producing music as a hobby as a new challenge. He progressed and offered services, but services are on hiatus after his son’s birth. Some services he used to provide included record engineering, mixing engineering, and DJing for small dances and events. Kalvin also has released music on YouTube. He has one song available; he has more songs in the works. Kalvin plans to keep improving with his music aspirations. Kalvin also likes to support local artists like King Iso from Omaha, Nebraska. He also enjoys listening to Ekoh, Phix, Russ, Juice WRLD, Mac Miller, and HonestAv. You can find Kalvin’s song, Toxic, on the Home page under “What I’ve been up to.”
